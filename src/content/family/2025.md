title: "The Year of Settling In"
---

2025 has been a year of roots growing deeper.

We moved into our place in New York and slowly made it feel like home. The kids started at a new school and, after a rocky few weeks, found their rhythm. Weekend mornings became our favorite time — unhurried breakfasts, walks to the park, nowhere we needed to be.

We traveled less this year, and it was exactly what we needed. A quiet summer upstate. A few long weekends visiting family. The kind of year where the best moments were ordinary ones.

Looking forward to what 2026 brings.
