This is the beginning of something small and intentional.

I've been building things for years — products, teams, routines — but I've rarely written about any of it. That changes now. Not because I have grand things to say, but because writing is how I make sense of what I'm learning.

This site is deliberately simple. No analytics, no newsletter popups, no comments section. Just words on a page.

I'll write about what I'm building, what I'm reading, and what I'm figuring out as a father and a maker. Some of it will be useful. Most of it will just be honest.

If you're here, thanks for reading.
